About Us

Zogaan

Zogaan is a Bangladesh based platform for independent content creators or small groups aross the internet (Youtube, Instagram, personal blogs and websites) to get paid and supported by their patrons and supporters.

  • Pledge to your favorite artists, interact with them and get a closer look behind thier work.
  • Subscribe and stay up to date with their latest news, upcoming projects and regular updates.
  • Support your favorite artists, so they can keep producing their content for their fans.

We provide an extensive set of tools for a content creator to reach out to their audience, and interact with them, providing incentive for fans to support them in their efforts to produce better content. A fan can pledge to their favorite artist, or subscribe for recurring content, or just buy their content directly.

Services

What we do offer

Pledges

Pledges are a way for a fan to directly support an artist, to show their solidarity and appreciation for their favorites. They may choose to remain anonymous, or interact directly with an artist through text.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are monthly and a way to keep up to date with an artist through blog posts, updates, a news portal and videos. A sneak peak to upcoming content is also provided, subject to the artist's wishes.

Direct Content

Artists can choose to sell their content directly, should they desire to garner support from their fans only through content as opposed to subscription and pledges or perhaps in tandem wih both.

Secure Transactions

We use our own method of transactions that utilizes and brings together numerous forms of online payment for easy accesibility for everyone and ensures security and ease of use.

Encoded Payments

All payments are encoded, and thus adding an extra layer of security as well as making it easier for the end user to confirm their transaction, eliminating the need for clunky and hard to remember codes.

Automated Payments

The entire process of payments and transactions is automated, making it easier for the artist to collect, as well as for a fan to pay, as incorrect payments are automatically given back, and an artist is automatically paid their part at the end of the month.
